━━ FAQ — The Real Questions ━━

You're thinking it. We answered it.

How far in advance should I book?+

3+ weeks is the sweet spot. School holidays and December book out 6–8 weeks in advance. The faster you submit, the more dates you can choose from.

What if it rains?+

We'll work with you on a backup indoor location. Light showers? We perform anyway. Sydney storm? We reschedule at no cost. Wet weather is never your problem.

How many kids can you handle?+

From 6 to 250. We've done backyard birthdays and full church family days. Anything over 40 kids we recommend a second entertainer at +$200.

Are your entertainers safe?+

Every entertainer holds a current Working With Children Check (WWCC), is fully public-liability insured ($20M), and has been background-checked by us personally. Documentation provided on request.

What ages do you cater for?+

Best fit is 4–12. We adjust the show, language and games to match the age of the birthday kid. We've done both 4-year-old preschoolers and 11-year-old tweens — same package, different energy.

Can I be involved on the day?+

Encouraged but never required. Some parents jump in the games. Others sit down for the first time in a month and let us run the entire show. Both are great.

Is the Gospel message preachy?+

No. It's woven into the games and character so naturally that even non-Christian guests love it. We've had Hindu, Muslim and atheist families book us back-to-back. The message is hope, kindness, and Jesus' love — never pressure.

What if my child is shy?+

We've trained for this. Our entertainers always include — never spotlight — quiet kids. By minute 15, even the shy ones are usually leading a game.

Can I see references?+

Send us an email. We'll connect you with three Sydney parents who booked us in the last 90 days. They'll tell you the truth, even the things we wouldn't.

What's actually included for $499?+

Everything in the value stack above. No upsells on the day, no surprise fees, no 'oh by the way that's extra'. You sign once. We deliver. Done.
