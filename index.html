Frequently Asked

The questions parents ask us most.

What does a standard event look like?+

A mix of imaginative games and faith-based themes pointing to biblical truths. Shows feature characters and role-play designed to connect kids with Jesus's life-changing message — through play they already love.

What ages is this suitable for?+

Ages 4–12 is the sweet spot. We adjust the show, language, and games to match the age of the birthday child.

How many children can you cater for?+

From small backyard parties to full church and school events. There's no strict limit, but additional adult supervision is recommended for larger groups.

What characters and themes can I choose from?+

Sergeant Order, Agent Insight, Captain Kindheart, and Jumpin' Joey — with customisation based on your child's interests when needed.

Can I be involved on the day?+

Encouraged but optional. Parental involvement sets a beautiful example, but your entertainer can run the full performance independently with general supervision.

Where can I host an event?+

Home, church, school, or any other suitable Sydney location. Venue guidance available if you need a hand finding one.

How far in advance should I book?+

3 weeks in advance is the sweet spot. For holiday-season bookings (school holidays, December), submit your expression of interest as early as possible — those dates fill quickly.

What if it rains?+

We recommend planning a backup indoor location. Light showers are manageable, but severe weather requires advance planning — we'll work through it with you.

What services don't you provide?+

We don't handle event decorations, catering, or venue bookings. Guidance is available, but we focus on the show so it's the best it can possibly be.

Is the Gospel message preachy?+

Never. The Gospel is woven through the games and characters so naturally that even families who aren't Christian feel welcomed and warmed by it. The message is hope, kindness, and Jesus' love — never pressure.
